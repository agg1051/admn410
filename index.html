History

After becoming a veterinary technician, Lexi Gonzalez discovered there were not many sustainable animal companies. So, she decided to create her own. In 2020, Lexi's Pet Products sold their first pet product, biodegradeable waste bags. The product was an instant success. Lexi's then grew to sell more eco-friendly products!

Why Use Us?

Lexi's only has the safest products for your pet. All our employees have veterinary knowledge and experience.
